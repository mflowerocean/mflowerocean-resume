Summary

Through my work and practices as a teacher and artist, I have a combined twenty years of on the job experience. I have taught children, adults with developmental differences, senior citizens, and more. I have ten years of performance choreagraphy and organizational achievements. I understand the need for teachers to embrace technological developments and how much technological fields can benefit from the creativity of teachers. I wish to work in an company where I can use and grow my talents as an artist and my interests in web and technological development for the benefit of humanity creative problems.
